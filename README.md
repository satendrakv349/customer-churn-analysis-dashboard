# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn patterns and identifying the key factors that influence customer attrition. Using Python for Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Power BI for visualization, the project provides actionable insights that can help businesses improve customer retention and reduce revenue loss.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer churn behavior.
- Identify high-risk customers.
- Discover major reasons behind customer attrition.
- Evaluate the impact of contract types, payment methods, tenure, and internet services on churn.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### Insight 1

Customers with **Month-to-Month contracts** exhibit significantly higher churn compared to customers on longer-term contracts.

### Insight 2

Customers who churn have higher average monthly charges than retained customers.

### Insight 3

Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to leave the service.

### Insight 4

Electronic Check users show the highest churn among payment methods.

### Insight 5

Fiber Optic customers contribute a large portion of churned customers.

### Insight 6

Senior Citizens demonstrate a relatively higher churn tendency compared to non-senior customers.

---

## 📊 Results

- Churn Rate: ~26.5%
- High churn concentration among Month-to-Month customers.
- Revenue loss strongly associated with customer attrition.
- Contract type, tenure, and payment method are major churn drivers.
